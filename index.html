The Game is Afoot!

The cats have decided to procrastinate by playing a friendly game of war. Let's see who is victorious.

How to Play

  1. Name your cats
  2. Push the button to let them have at it!
  3. Whoever wins the round gets both cards, but watch out for a war!
  4. Push the button or press "enter" again to let them play another round. Cats are scrappy, so this might take awhile...
  5. The cat with all the cards wins!
